About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Re-Bing Wu. I received my B.Eng. degree in Automation from Beihang University (2020) and my M.Eng. degree from Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School (2023).

My research lies at the intersection of control theory and quantum physics. I am particularly interested in developing robust quantum control methods for quantum information processing, and in leveraging machine learning techniques—especially reinforcement learning—to tackle quantum control problems under model uncertainties and decoherence.

我是清华大学自动化系博士生，师从吴热冰教授。 我的研究方向是量子控制与量子信息， 致力于开发针对量子信息处理的鲁棒量子控制方法， 并将强化学习等机器学习技术应用于存在不确定性与退相干的量子控制问题。
